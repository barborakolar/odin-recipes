Cosmopolitan

Description

"I'd like a Cheeseburger, please, a large fries and a Cosmopolitan" [Carrie]

Ingredients

  • 40ml Vodka
  • 15ml Cointreau
  • 15ml Lemon Juice
  • 30ml Cranberry Juice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients in a Boston glass with cubed ice.
  2. Shake and fine strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
