White Russian

Description

The Dude likes to drink White Russians, which is a drink made of Vodka, Kahlua, and Cream. The first time we see the Dude he's buying cream at Ralph's, which he pays for by personalized cheque (the background of the cheque appears to be a watercolor painting of a Blue Whale). At Maude's studio, The Dude can be seen using a powdered creamer in substitute of the cream.

At Jackie Treehorn's house, The Dude calls the drink a Caucasian when he starts to feel the effects of the sedative put in his drink.

Ingredients

  • 40ml Vodka
  • 20ml Kahlua
  • Milk until filling glass
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Pour the vodka and kahlua into a rocks glass over cubed ice.
  2. Stir gently until the drink is completely mixed.
  3. Float the milk on top of the mixed liquor until filling the glass completely.
  4. Let the milk mix through the ingredients and then serve.
