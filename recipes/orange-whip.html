Orange Whip

Description

The 1980s film 'The Blues Brothers' features many a treasured gem, with outrageous stunts, hilarious lines, and incredible performances. Among the film's best moments is one infamous line about an unfamiliar drink: the Orange Whip.

'Who wants an Orange Whip? Orange Whip? Orange Whip? Three Orange Whips!'

Ingredients

  • 30ml Vodka
  • 30ml White Rum
  • 15ml Triple Sec
  • 120ml Fresh Orange Juice
  • 60ml Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker or cup with crushed ice, and shake to mix.
  2. Pour into a Collins glass of your choice and garnish with an orange wheel.
